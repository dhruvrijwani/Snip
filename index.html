Your 6-Month Roadmap

From Beginner to Professional

A structured two-phase journey designed to build both technical mastery and real-world confidence.

Phase 1 · Months 1–3

Professional Training

Classroom to technique mastery

  • Consultation & Styling — understanding hair types and client consultations
  • Women's Haircuts — Long Hair (4 weeks), Mid-length (2 weeks), Short (2 weeks)
  • Men's Haircuts — modern and classic grooming techniques
  • Basic Color Theory — science of application with L'Oréal Professionnel support
  • Model Practice Workshops — hands-on training with real models
Phase 2 · Months 4–6

Internship at Snip

From classroom to salon floor

  • Work alongside experienced stylists in a live salon environment
  • Serve real clients under professional supervision
  • Build your portfolio and client interaction skills
  • Develop professional conduct and salon etiquette
  • Complete your journey with a professional hairdresser certificate

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Will I receive a certification upon completion? +
Yes. Upon successfully completing your 3-month training and 3-month internship, you will receive a professional certificate from Snip Academy of Hair and Makeup — a recognized credential that validates your expertise.
Can I work as a hairstylist after the course? +
Absolutely. After finishing the program, you will be fully equipped to pursue career paths including:
  • Joining the professional styling team at Snip
  • Working in high-end salons across the country
  • Starting your own independent salon or studio
  • Working in hair for film, television, and advertising
  • Designing looks for fashion editorials and magazine features
Do I need prior experience to join? +
No. This course is specifically designed for beginners. We take you through everything from the very basics of hair science and client consultations to advanced cutting and coloring, ensuring you build a strong professional foundation from scratch.
Is the job placement really guaranteed? +
Yes. We offer guaranteed placements at Snip for deserving candidates who demonstrate dedication, professional conduct, and skill during their training and internship period.
What is the "Model Practice" part of the course? +
Practical training is a core part of the Snip Academy experience. You will have dedicated weeks to practice on real models provided by the academy — giving you hands-on training and the chance to build interpersonal skills by consulting with people you're unfamiliar with.
Are there flexible payment options? +
Yes. The total fee is ₹1,50,000, which covers your 3 months of training and 3-month internship. We offer flexible payment plans to help manage the investment — our team will walk you through the options when you enrol.
