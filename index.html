Professional Training

  • Consultation & Styling: Understanding hair types, client consultations, and basic styling techniques.

Frequently Asked Questions

Will I receive a certification upon completion of the course?+
Yes. Upon successfully completing your 3-month training and the 3-month internship, you will receive a professional certificate from Snip Academy of Hair and Makeup. This is a recognized credential that validates your expertise and helps you start your career as a qualified professional.
Can I work as a hairstylist after completing the course?+
Absolutely. After finishing the program, you will be fully equipped to pursue several career paths, including:
  • Joining the professional styling team at Snip
  • Working in high-end salons across the country
  • Starting your own independent salon or studio
  • Working in the hair department for film, television, and advertising
  • Designing looks for fashion editorials and magazine features
Do I need prior experience to join?+
No. This course is specifically designed for beginners. We take you through everything from the very basics of hair science and client consultations to advanced cutting and coloring, ensuring you build a strong professional foundation from scratch.
How is the course structured?+
The structure of each week is highly practical. You will move from theory into intensive blocks for women's and men's haircuts, color and chemical services like straightening. The goal is to ensure you spend as much time as possible practicing techniques to prepare you for the salon floor.
What is the "Model Practice" part of the course?+
Practical training is a core part of the Snip Academy experience. You will have dedicated weeks to practice on real models provided by the academy. This gives you hands-on training and the chance to brush up on your interpersonal skills by talking to and consulting with people you aren't familiar with.
Is the job placement really guaranteed?+
Yes. We offer guaranteed placements at Snip for deserving candidates who show dedication, professional conduct, and skill during their 3 months of training and 3-month internship.
What is the total fee for the course?+
The certified course fee is ₹1,50,000. This covers your 3 months of intensive training and your 3-month internship at Snip. We also offer flexible payment plans to help manage the investment.
How can I enroll?+
The best way to start is to schedule a consultation. Share your details in the form at the end and speak with our team about upcoming batch dates and the enrollment process.
